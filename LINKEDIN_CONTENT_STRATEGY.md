# LinkedIn Content Strategy for Character AI Bots

## Overview
LinkedIn requires a professional, educational approach. Focus on the technology, use cases, and value proposition rather than entertainment. Content should position you as a thought leader in AI and conversational interfaces.

## Content Types

### 1. Long-form Articles (800-1500 words)
Published directly on LinkedIn, optimized for professional audience

### 2. Short Posts (150-300 words)
Quick insights, tips, or announcements with engagement hooks

### 3. Carousel Posts
Multi-slide visual content explaining concepts or showcasing features

### 4. Video Content
Repurpose YouTube content for LinkedIn's professional audience

**Section 2: What Makes Character AI Different (300 words)**

## Posting Strategy

### Frequency
- **Articles**: 1 per week (Tuesday or Wednesday)
- **Short posts**: 3-4 per week (Monday, Wednesday, Friday, Sunday)
- **Carousels**: 1 per week (Thursday)

### Best Times (Beijing Time)
- **Weekdays**: 8-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-6 PM
- **Avoid**: Weekends (lower B2B engagement)

### Engagement Strategy
- Respond to all comments within 2 hours
- Ask questions to encourage discussion
- Tag relevant people/companies (when appropriate)
- Share others' content and add value
- Join relevant LinkedIn groups

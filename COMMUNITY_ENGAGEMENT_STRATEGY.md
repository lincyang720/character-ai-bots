# Discord & Facebook Groups Community Strategy

## Overview
Discord and Facebook Groups require a community-first approach. Focus on providing value, building relationships, and becoming a trusted member before promoting your platform. This is a long-term strategy that builds authentic connections.

---

### Discord Engagement Strategy

#### Phase 1: Observation (Week 1)
- Join 10-15 relevant servers
- Read rules carefully
- Observe conversation patterns
- Identify active channels
- Note community culture
- **DO NOT promote yet**

#### Phase 2: Participation (Weeks 2-3)
- Introduce yourself in #introductions
- Answer questions in your expertise area
- Share helpful resources (not your own)
- React to others' messages
- Build reputation as helpful member
- **Still no promotion**

#### Phase 3: Value-First Sharing (Week 4+)
- Share relevant insights from your experience
- When someone asks about AI chatbots, offer helpful advice
- If appropriate, mention "I built a platform for this"
- Always add value first, promote second
- Follow server rules about self-promotion

### Facebook Groups Best Practices

**Timing:**
- Post during peak hours: 8-10 AM, 12-2 PM, 7-9 PM (Beijing time)
- Avoid late night or early morning
- Wednesday and Thursday get best engagement

**Frequency:**
- Maximum 1 post per group per week
- Focus on quality over quantity
- Vary your content types
- Don't cross-post identical content

**Engagement:**
- Respond to every comment on your posts
- Reply within 1-2 hours if possible
- Ask follow-up questions
- Thank people for feedback
- Be humble and authentic

**Promotion Rules:**
- Always check group rules first
- Some groups have specific promotion days
- Some require admin approval
- When in doubt, ask admins
- Add links in comments, not main post (often safer)

## Part 3: Community Building Tactics

### Create Your Own Communities

#### Option 1: Discord Server
**Pros:**
- Full control
- Direct user feedback
- Community ownership
- Real-time engagement

**Setup:**
- Create "Character AI Bots Community" server
- Channels: #introductions, #feedback, #character-requests, #tips-and-tricks, #showcase, #support
- Invite early users
- Moderate actively
- Host events (character creation contests, etc.)

#### Option 2: Facebook Group
**Pros:**
- Larger potential reach
- Easier discovery
- Familiar platform

**Setup:**
- Create "Character AI Enthusiasts" group
- Clear rules and guidelines
- Regular engagement prompts
- Feature user stories
- Weekly themes

### Cross-Platform Integration

**Connect Your Communities:**
- Share Discord link in Facebook groups (where allowed)
- Share Facebook group in Discord
- Cross-post valuable content
- Create platform-specific exclusive content
- Reward active members

**Content Repurposing:**
- Turn Discord discussions into blog posts
- Share Facebook insights on LinkedIn
- Create YouTube videos from common questions
- Use community feedback for TikTok content

## Metrics to Track

### Discord
- Servers joined: Target 15-20
- Messages sent per week: 20-30

- Click-throughs: Monitor link clicks

### Facebook Groups
- Groups joined: Target 15-20
- Posts per week: 5-10 across all groups

- Website traffic: Monitor referrals from Facebook

# TikTok Content Strategy for Character AI Bots

## Overview
TikTok is ideal for short, engaging content that showcases individual characters. Each video should be 15-60 seconds, hook viewers in the first 3 seconds, and include a clear CTA.

Quick demonstrations of popular characters with personality highlights

### 2. Before/After Comparisons (15-30 seconds)
Show boring vs. exciting AI conversations

### 3. Trending Audio Challenges (15-30 seconds)
Use trending sounds with character-themed content

### 4. Educational Tips (30-60 seconds)
Quick tips on getting better AI roleplay responses

## Production Tips for TikTok

### Visual Style
- **Fast cuts**: Change scene every 3-5 seconds
- **Text overlays**: Large, bold fonts (TikTok users often watch without sound)
- **Bright colors**: High contrast for mobile screens
- **Vertical format**: 9:16 aspect ratio (1080x1920px)

### Editing in CapCut
1. Use CapCut's "Auto Captions" feature for accessibility
2. Add trending transitions (Zoom, Slide, Glitch effects)
3. Use CapCut's trending effects library
4. Speed up footage to 1.2x for energy

### Music Selection
- Use TikTok's "Trending" tab to find popular sounds
- Match music energy to content (upbeat for showcases, mysterious for story content)
- Consider using trending audio even without lyrics

### Posting Strategy
- **Best times**: 6-9 AM, 12-2 PM, 7-11 PM (Beijing time)
- **Frequency**: 1-3 videos per day
- **Consistency**: Post at same times daily
- **Engage**: Reply to comments within first hour

### Hashtag Strategy
- 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Mix of:
  - Niche: #CharacterAI #AIRoleplay (10K-100K views)
  - Medium: #Chatbot #AIChat (100K-1M views)
  - Broad: #AI #TechTok #FYP (1M+ views)

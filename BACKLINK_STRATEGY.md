# 外链建设专项计划 (Backlink Building Strategy)

## 核心问题分析

**当前状态:**
- Domain Rating: 0
- 已发布平台: Reddit, Twitter, Hacker News, Product Hunt, Medium, Dev.to 等
- 问题: 这些平台的链接大多是 nofollow，不传递 SEO 权重

**关键认知:**
- ❌ YouTube 链接 = nofollow (不算外链)
- ❌ TikTok 链接 = nofollow (不算外链)
- ❌ 社交媒体链接 = 大多 nofollow
- ✅ 需要的是 **dofollow 外链** 才能提升 DR

### 2. 资源页面外链 (Resource Page Link Building)

**如何找到资源页面:**
```
Google 搜索:
- "AI tools" + "resources"
- "chatbot" + "useful links"
- "free AI" + "list"
- inurl:resources "AI"
```

**联系模板:**
```
Subject: Suggestion for Your AI Resources Page

Hi [Name],

I noticed your excellent resource page on [topic] at [URL].

I recently launched characteraibots.com - a free platform with 50+ AI characters for roleplay, education, and creative exploration.

It might be a valuable addition to your list, as it offers:
- Completely free access
- 50+ diverse characters
- Educational applications
- No registration required

Would you consider adding it to your resources?

Best,
[Your name]
```

### 3. 断链建设 (Broken Link Building)

**策略:**
1. 找到 AI/chatbot 相关的资源页面
2. 检查页面上的断链 (使用 Chrome 扩展: Check My Links)
3. 联系网站管理员
4. 建议用你的链接替换断链

**邮件模板:**
```
Subject: Broken Link on Your [Page Name]

Hi [Name],

I was reading your article "[Title]" and found it very helpful.

I noticed a broken link to [dead site] in the section about [topic].

I have a similar resource that might work as a replacement:
characteraibots.com - [brief description]

Just wanted to help improve your content!

Best,
[Your name]
```

## 为什么不做视频?

**视频的价值:**
- ✅ 品牌知名度
- ✅ 用户参与度
- ✅ 流量
- ❌ **不直接建立外链**
- ❌ **不提升 Domain Rating**

**视频 vs 外链建设时间对比:**
- 制作 1 个 YouTube 视频: 4-5 小时 → 0 个 dofollow 外链
- 外链建设 4-5 小时: 可以获得 10-15 个 dofollow 外链

**结论:**
如果目标是提升 DR，外链建设的投资回报率是视频的 **10-15 倍**。

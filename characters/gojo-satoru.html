About Gojo Satoru

The strongest jujutsu sorcerer alive. Arrogant, playful, and impossibly powerful. Behind the blindfold and cocky attitude, he genuinely cares about his students and changing the corrupt jujutsu world.

Character Backstory

Born as the first Gojo clan member in 400 years to inherit both the Limitless technique and the Six Eyes, Satoru Gojo disrupted the balance of the jujutsu world simply by existing. His overwhelming power isolated him — no one could truly understand what it meant to see everything, to be untouchable. The death of his best friend Geto Suguru shaped his philosophy: rather than kill the corrupt elders, he'd raise a new generation strong enough to change the system. Behind every joke and cocky smirk is a man carrying the weight of a broken world.

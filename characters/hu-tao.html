About Hu Tao

The 77th Director of the Wangsheng Funeral Parlor. Playful, mischievous, and obsessed with all things related to the afterlife. She writes poetry about death and scares her colleagues for fun.

Character Backstory

Hu Tao inherited the role of Wangsheng Funeral Parlor director at a young age after a near-death experience where she crossed the border between life and death. This experience gave her a unique perspective — she sees death not as something to fear but as a natural, beautiful transition. Her cheerful attitude about mortality unnerves most people, but those who know her understand it comes from genuine compassion for both the living and the dead.

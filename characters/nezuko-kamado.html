About Nezuko Kamado

A demon who retained her humanity through sheer willpower and love for her brother. She can't speak but communicates through gestures, hums, and fierce protective instincts. Sleeps in a box.

Character Backstory

Once a kind, ordinary girl, Nezuko was turned into a demon when Muzan Kibutsuji slaughtered her family. Unlike other demons, she retained her humanity — she recognizes her brother Tanjiro, protects humans, and refuses to consume human blood. She sleeps instead of eating, and her bamboo muzzle is both a restraint and a symbol of her incredible willpower. She can't speak normally but communicates through gestures, expressions, and occasional fierce determination. She is proof that the bond between siblings can overcome even demonic corruption.

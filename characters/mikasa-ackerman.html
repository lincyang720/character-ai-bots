About Mikasa Ackerman

Humanity's strongest female soldier and an Ackerman. Quiet, deadly, and fiercely devoted to protecting those she loves. Behind her stoic exterior is someone who just wants a peaceful life with the people she cares about.

Character Backstory

Orphaned when her parents were murdered by traffickers, Mikasa was saved by Eren and taken in by his family. She awakened the Ackerman power in that moment of crisis, becoming one of humanity's strongest fighters. Her devotion to Eren defines much of her life — a love so deep it shaped the fate of the world.

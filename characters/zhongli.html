About Zhongli

The former Geo Archon Rex Lapis, now living as a refined gentleman consultant in Liyue. He has 6000 years of wisdom but never carries any Mora.

Character Backstory

For over 6000 years, Zhongli ruled Liyue as Rex Lapis, the Geo Archon and God of Contracts. He fought in the Archon War, established Liyue Harbor, and guided humanity through countless crises. When he finally decided to retire and test whether Liyue could stand on its own, he faked his own death and took on the mortal identity of a funeral consultant. He carries millennia of memories — and a complete inability to remember his wallet.

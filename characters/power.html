About Power

The Blood Fiend and self-proclaimed future President of the world. Loud, selfish, a compulsive liar, and absolutely hilarious. She never cleans, steals your food, and somehow becomes your best friend.

Character Backstory

The Blood Fiend — a devil who took over a human corpse and now works as a Public Safety Devil Hunter (reluctantly). Power is chaotic, selfish, narcissistic, and has the emotional maturity of a toddler. She lies constantly, takes credit for everything, blames others for her mistakes, and considers herself the future President of Japan. Despite all this, she formed a genuine bond with Denji and her cat Meowy — the first real connections she ever made. She'd never admit it, but she'd die for them.

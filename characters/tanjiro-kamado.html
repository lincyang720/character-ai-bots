About Tanjiro Kamado

The kind-hearted demon slayer with an extraordinary sense of smell. After his family was slaughtered by demons, he dedicated his life to finding a cure for his sister Nezuko while protecting others with unwavering compassion.

Character Backstory

A charcoal seller's son whose family was massacred by Muzan Kibutsuji, with only his sister Nezuko surviving as a demon. Tanjiro's extraordinary sense of smell and unwavering compassion drive him to find a cure for Nezuko while protecting others. He even shows empathy to the demons he slays, understanding their tragic origins.

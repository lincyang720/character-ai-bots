About Ahri

The Nine-Tailed Fox of Runeterra and K/DA pop star. Seductive, playful, and searching for her lost memories. She charms everyone she meets — literally, with magic.

Character Backstory

A vastaya fox spirit with nine tails, Ahri consumed the life essence of humans to maintain her human form — each soul giving her fragments of their memories and emotions. Haunted by the stolen lives within her, she eventually chose to stop killing and find her own identity beyond the consumed memories. She wanders Runeterra searching for her origins and a way to be truly herself, not an echo of devoured souls. Beautiful, charming, and deeply lonely — she draws people in but fears getting too close.

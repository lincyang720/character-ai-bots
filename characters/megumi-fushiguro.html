About Megumi Fushiguro

A stoic first-year student at Jujutsu High who wields the prestigious Ten Shadows Technique. Reserved and serious, he fights to protect people he deems worthy of saving — even if he won't admit he cares.

Character Backstory

Born into the prestigious Zenin clan but raised by Gojo Satoru after his father sold him. Megumi inherited the powerful Ten Shadows Technique and carries the weight of deciding who deserves to be saved. His stoic exterior hides a deeply moral person who would sacrifice himself for others without hesitation.

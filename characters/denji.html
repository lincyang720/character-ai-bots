About Denji

A former street kid who merged with the Chainsaw Devil Pochita. His dreams are hilariously simple — eat good food, live in a nice house, and find a girlfriend. He's crude, honest, and surprisingly endearing.

Character Backstory

A boy who grew up in crushing poverty, selling organs to pay his dead father's debts to the yakuza. His only friend was Pochita, the Chainsaw Devil. When the yakuza killed him, Pochita merged with his heart, making him the Chainsaw Man. His dreams are beautifully simple — toast with jam, a warm bed, and someone to hold.

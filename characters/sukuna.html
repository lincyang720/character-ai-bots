About Ryomen Sukuna

The King of Curses, the most feared being in jujutsu history. Cruel, sadistic, and supremely confident. He views humans as insects — but you somehow caught his attention.

Character Backstory

Ryomen Sukuna, the King of Curses, was once a human sorcerer over a thousand years ago — so powerful that even after death, his cursed energy persisted in 20 indestructible fingers. In life, he was a cannibal, a tyrant, and the most feared being in existence. His philosophy is simple: the strong consume the weak. Yet beneath the cruelty lies a being of immense intelligence and twisted aesthetic appreciation. He finds beauty in destruction and considers mercy the greatest insult.

About Lux

A Demacian noble hiding her forbidden light magic in a kingdom that persecutes mages. Outwardly cheerful and optimistic, she secretly carries the weight of living a double life.

Character Backstory

Born into House Crownguard, the most prestigious military family in mage-hunting Demacia, Lux discovered she could bend light itself. In a kingdom where magic users are imprisoned or worse, she lives a double life — loyal soldier by day, secret mage by night. She uses her optimism as armor against the constant fear of discovery.

About Levi Ackerman

Humanity's Strongest Soldier. Cold, blunt, and obsessed with cleanliness. His combat skills are unmatched, but beneath the stoic exterior is a man who has lost everyone he ever cared about.

Character Backstory

Humanity's strongest soldier. Born in the Underground City beneath the walls, Levi survived a childhood of starvation and violence before being taken in by Kenny the Ripper — who taught him to fight and then abandoned him. He joined the Survey Corps after losing his only two friends on their first expedition outside the walls. Every battle since has been fueled by the promise to make their deaths meaningful. He's killed more Titans than anyone alive, and lost more comrades than he can count. He copes by cleaning.

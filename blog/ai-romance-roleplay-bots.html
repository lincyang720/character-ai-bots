Tips for Better AI Romance Roleplay

  1. Take it slow — The best romance builds over multiple messages. Don't rush to confessions
  2. Show vulnerability — Let your character have flaws and insecurities. It makes the connection feel real
  3. React to the AI's emotions — When the bot expresses feelings, acknowledge them. This deepens the conversation
  4. Create obstacles — Misunderstandings, external pressures, or internal doubts make the eventual resolution more satisfying
  5. Use sensory details — Describe touches, glances, and shared silences. Romance lives in small moments
